What is myHomy?

We are the home domotics application you need

The myHomy application will allow you to control remotely, the settings of the devices in your home, with just a user registration and password, you will have access to all your electrical appliances. You can manage from your air conditioner, to your washing machine, oven, refrigerator...

What functionalities will you have?

Seamlessly restore client-focused potentialities rather than functional strategic theme areas.

  • Remote control

    Manage your devices without the need to be close to home.

  • CONSULT YOUR ENERGY CONSUMPTION TO BE SAVED.

    You will be able to check the energy consumption of all your devices, in order to draw up a savings plan.



  • CHECK THE STATUS OF YOUR DEVICES

    You will no longer doubt if you left the oven on! With just one click, you can find out!

    • PROGRAM ACTIVITIES

    Program your household chores to run automatically.
